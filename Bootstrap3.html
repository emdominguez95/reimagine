The Witness

The Witness is a beautiful open world puzzle game where you have to learn the rules of the puzzles with no instructions.

Kya: Dark Lineage

Kya: Dark Lineage is an old action adventure game about a girl who falls into another world. It's a classic from 2003 that no one I know has ever heard of, but my siblings and I loved growing up.

Minecraft

Minecraft is an open world snadbox game where you can build anything your heart desires. There are also survival mechanics and cute square animals.

Overwatch

Overwatch is a team-based first-person shooter. It's heavily stylized and futuristic, with each character having unique superpowers.

Undertale

Undertale is an 8-bit style role-playing game featuring pacifist solutions to fights with "monsters" and a pervasive sense of humor.

Beyond Good and Evil

Beyond Good and Evil is an action-adventure set on an alien planet with. It has a compelling story featuring a diverse alien cast. Sequel coming soon!
